Free Quotes

How to get a free quote

No forms, no phone trees — it works the simple way:

  1. Bring it in

    Walk in any weekday between 8 and 5 — no appointment needed — or call (860) 449-9514 to set a time.

  2. We inspect & call you

    We look the vehicle over and call you with what's wrong and what it costs, explained in plain English.

  3. You decide

    No work starts until you approve it. All forms of payment accepted — cash, card, or check.

Waiting on a repair? For most jobs the easiest option is a drop-off — we call you the moment it's ready. If you'd rather stay close, there's a convenience store connected to the building and Poquonnock Park nearby.
