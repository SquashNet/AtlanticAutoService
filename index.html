Know the price before we start

No mystery bills, no surprise line items. Every job at Atlantic starts the same way:

  1. Bring it in

    No appointment needed — walk in Monday through Friday, 8 to 5. Prefer to plan ahead? Call (860) 449-9514 and we'll set a time.

Why Atlantic

The way a shop should treat you

You'll know the cost up front

Free quotes on every job: we inspect, we call, we explain what's wrong and what it costs. Work starts when you say so — and if it doesn't need fixing, we won't tell you it does.

OEM parts — and we keep your old ones

Factory-spec parts on every repair, never cheap aftermarket substitutes. Your replaced parts stay at the shop so you can see exactly what came off your car.

Backed for a full year

Parts and labor covered 100% for 365 days or 4,000 miles, whichever comes first. The terms are printed on every estimate and invoice.
